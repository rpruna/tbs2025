🚫 Step Away from the Dog-Washing Tunnel!

Hey pack leaders! Pauline here. I’ve seen some wild contraptions in my travels, but nothing says “ruff day” like a dog being sent through a whirring shampoo conveyor of doom. Sure, it looks efficient on TikTok, but it’s basically a spin cycle of panic for your pooch. Let’s keep tails wagging — not spinning!

Try these human-approved, canine-approved alternatives instead:

  • DIY Spa Day: A kiddie pool + lukewarm water is a five-star experience compared to Tunnel Terror 3000.
  • Treat Distractors: Smear peanut butter on the tub wall. Your dog licks, you lather. It’s teamwork!
  • Shampoo Shuffle: Use a sulfate-free, pH-balanced dog shampoo. Your pup’s coat will shine brighter than your future IPO.
  • The Burrito Burr: Post-wash, wrap your dog in a fluffy towel burrito. Extra snugs, zero dryer noise.
  • Keep It Short & Silly: Sing terribly. Dance badly. The more ridiculous you are, the calmer they’ll be. Science probably says so.

Remember: if it feels like you’re putting your best friend through an automatic car wash, you’re barking up the wrong routine. 😉

Stay waggy,

💌 Pauline “Two Treats, No Tricks” Rivera
